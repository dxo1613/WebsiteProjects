My Journey

I have decided to do a website on Sequoia National Park in order to expose you to the amazing scenery and adventure you can have at this park. I have gone camping here a couple of times and every single time I go I am impressed by the sheer pristine beauty of the park.

As an avid lover of the outdoors I can say by far of all the parks that I have visited this is my favorite one. It is located in northern California about a four hour drive from my home. The giant sequoia tree's are a sight to behold and the beauty of this park creates a soothing and relaxing atmosphere. Hopefully you consider visitng this park someday as my words can only describe the beauty of this park in it's simplest form.
