Yosemite National Park

There are a couple of parks that surround Sequoia National Park that are also worth a visit this is just an overview of Yosemite National Park.


Yosemite National Park is one of the largest and least fragmented habitats. Over 95% of the park is designated as wilderness. The park is home to the world famous Steamboat geyser, the world's tallest active geyser. The park is characterized by the tall granite rocks and rock climbing is a popular recreation activity here.


The world famous steamboat geyser erupting. It erupts frequently shooting mineral rich water into the air as far as 300 feet.



An image of the U shaped valley of Yosemite Park. The cliffs and valley were carved out by huge glaciers millions of year ago.
