This page highlights what its like camping. It gives an overview of some of the campsites and lodging that the park provides.

Buckeye Flats

Buckeye Flats is our usual camping spot. It is a relatvely hidden camping site as the entrance is obscured by a giant rock (Hosptial Rock) and the entrance is a very narrow road. In my opinion this is the best camping spot as their is a good amount of trails that allow you to see the surrounding area and the campsite overlooks the Kaweah River only a couple of feet away. A lot of people swim and fish at this campsite.



Potwisha Campgrounds

Potwisha Campgrounds is the first campground you see when you come into the park. It offers a lot of amenities but I don't like because it feels like a city to me because it has a high number of campground and so there tends to be a lot of people packed into here.



Azalea Campgrounds

We only camped here once because all the lower elevation campsites were taken. This campsite is located at a high altittude so it was very cold and we were not prepared for that when we went. This campsite is also suitable for RV's so there was aquite a bit of people were comfy but it was a cold night for us that day.
